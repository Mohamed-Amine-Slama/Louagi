# Louagi Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the rules, deadlines, and fee structures governing passenger and driver cancellations on the **Louagi** Platform.

---

## 1. Passenger-Initiated Cancellations

### 1.1 The 2-Hour Cancellation Window
- Passengers are entitled to cancel a confirmed booking up to **2 hours before** the ride's scheduled departure time.
- Cancellations requested **less than 2 hours** before departure are strictly prohibited. The Platform will block the action, and no refunds of any kind will be issued.

### 1.2 Refund Rules for Passenger Cancellations
- **Seat Fare**: The cost of the booked seat(s) is fully refunded to the passenger's original payment method.
- **Reservation Surcharge**: The flat **3.000 TND reservation fee is strictly non-refundable** under passenger-initiated cancellations. Only the seat fare is returned.

---

## 2. Driver-Initiated Cancellations

### 2.1 Driver Cancellation Restrictions
- Drivers may cancel a scheduled ride before departure.
- Drivers are **not permitted** to cancel any ride once it has been marked as **in progress** or **completed**.

### 2.2 Refund Rules for Driver Cancellations
- If a driver cancels a ride, **all passengers are automatically refunded in full**.
- The refund will include **100% of the seat fare** plus the **3.000 TND reservation surcharge**.

---

## 3. Administrative Cancellations (Overrides)
- Louagi Administrators have the authority to bypass the standard 2-hour passenger cancellation window to cancel a ride under exceptional circumstances (e.g., vehicle breakdown, passenger emergency, or security concerns).
- Administrative cancellations trigger a **full refund** (including seat fare and the 3.000 TND surcharge) to all affected passengers.

---

## 4. Refund Processing and Timeline
- Refunds are initiated immediately upon cancellation.
- The funds are credited back to the passenger's linked payment gateway account (Flouci or Konnect).
- Processing times are determined by the payment gateway and the passenger's card issuer, typically resolving within 1 to 5 business days.
- Transaction references for all cancellations and refund actions are recorded in the immutable system audit log.
