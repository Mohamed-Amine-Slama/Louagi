# Louagi Terms of Service

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

Welcome to **Louagi**, a modern platform facilitating inter-city louage (shared taxi) travel in Tunisia. By using our application, websites, or services (collectively, the "Platform"), you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service.

---

## 1. Description of Services
Louagi provides a technology platform that connects passengers looking to book seats in shared taxis (louages) with verified independent drivers operating licensed louages on specified routes within the Republic of Tunisia. Louagi does not provide transportation services and is not a transportation carrier.

---

## 2. Account Registration and Vetting

### 2.1 Passenger Accounts
- Passengers must register an account using their full name, a valid phone number, and an email address.
- A 6-digit OTP verification code sent via SMS is required to activate and log in to the account.

### 2.2 Driver Accounts
- Onboarding as a driver requires uploading a valid National ID card, a professional driver's license, and a photo of the vehicle.
- Driver accounts are registered in a **Pending Review** status.
- Access to publish rides and accept bookings is strictly restricted until a Louagi Administrator verifies and approves the submitted documents.

### 2.3 Account Suspension and Impersonation
- Louagi Administrators reserve the right to suspend or terminate user accounts for violating these terms, fraudulent bookings, or reports of misconduct.
- Admin support sessions may temporarily impersonate accounts for troubleshooting purposes, with all actions recorded in an immutable audit log.

---

## 3. Pricing, Fees, and Revenue Splits

### 3.1 Government-Set Fares
- In compliance with Tunisian transport authority regulations, fares are fixed per route.
- Drivers do not set or negotiate passenger seat pricing. Every ride on the same route charges the identical government-set base fare.

### 3.2 Reservation Surcharges
- Each seat booking incurs a flat **3.000 TND reservation surcharge** on top of the base seat fare.
- The 3 TND surcharge is automatically split as follows:
  - **2.000 TND** paid to the driver.
  - **1.000 TND** retained by the Louagi platform.

### 3.3 Payments and Card Tokenization
- Payments are processed securely via integration with mobile payment gateways (e.g., Flouci or Konnect).
- Louagi **never** stores full credit card or bank account details. All transaction data is tokenized by the payment gateway, and only transaction references are stored.

---

## 4. Bookings and Seat Locks

### 4.1 Atomic Seat Locks
- To prevent double-bookings, the Platform initiates an **atomic seat lock** for **10 minutes** when a passenger starts a booking.
- If the booking and payment are not completed within this 10-minute window, the lock automatically expires and the seats are released back to the general inventory.

### 4.2 Ticket Generation
- Upon successful payment, a confirmed ticket is generated with a unique reference number. 
- The ticket displays the ride details, driver name, seat count, and total paid price.

---

## 5. Cancellation and Refund Policy

### 5.1 Passenger Cancellations
- Passengers may cancel a confirmed booking through the Platform up to **2 hours** before the scheduled departure time.
- If cancelled more than 2 hours before departure, the passenger receives a refund of the base seat fare. The **3.000 TND reservation surcharge is strictly non-refundable**.
- Cancellations requested less than 2 hours before departure are **not permitted** and will be blocked by the Platform.

### 5.2 Driver Cancellations
- Drivers may cancel a scheduled ride before departure.
- In the event of a driver cancellation, **all passenger reservations are voided** and passengers receive a **full refund**, including the base seat fare and the 3.000 TND reservation surcharge.
- Drivers are strictly prohibited from cancelling rides that have been marked as **in progress** or **completed**.

## 6. Parcel Delivery Services (Boxes & Parcels)
- Users may book cargo slots for parcel delivery on participating rides.
- Delivery options are categorised into three tiers:
  1. **Standard**: Basic parcel transport.
  2. **Sensitive / Fragile**: Transport requiring additional care.
  3. **Critical / High Value**: Priority shipping for high-value items.
- Deliveries are priced at a fixed rate shown prior to booking.
- The sender is responsible for handing the parcel to the driver at the designated louage station and providing accurate recipient information.

## 7. Code of Conduct
- Both passengers and drivers agree to behave respectfully and professionally at louage stations and during transit.
- Drivers agree to maintain their vehicles in a safe, clean, and roadworthy condition.
- Passengers agree to arrive at the station prior to the scheduled departure time.
- Fraudulent booking attempts, deliberate double-booking, or abusive behavior will result in immediate suspension.
