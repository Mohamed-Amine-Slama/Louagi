# Louagi Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

At **Louagi**, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our Platform.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Passenger Accounts
- **Profile Information**: Name, phone number, email address, and preferred language.
- **Travel History**: Route queries, booking details, seats booked, and transaction histories.

### 1.2 Driver Accounts
- **Vetting Documents**: National ID card, Driver's License, and a photo of the vehicle.
- **Vehicle Information**: Brand, model, total seat capacity, and plate number.
- **Financial Details**: Flouci or Konnect account identifiers for weekly payouts.

### 1.3 Technical Logs
- **IP Addresses**: Captured for security and audit trail mapping.
- **Device Identifiers**: Captured to manage active login sessions.

---

## 2. How We Secure and Store Data

### 2.1 Field-Level Encryption
- Sensitive personally identifiable information (PII), including phone numbers and uploaded driver documents, is encrypted at the field level before being persisted to our database. This ensures data remains protected even in the event of unauthorized database access.

### 2.2 Payment Tokenization
- We do not store or process payment card details. All financial transactions are managed directly by tokenized payment gateways (Flouci/Konnect). Only the gateway-provided transaction references are stored.

### 2.3 Local Biometric Authentication
- The Platform supports Face ID and fingerprint sign-in via native device APIs.
- **Louagi does not collect, store, or transmit your biometric data.** Biometric verification is processed entirely locally on your device's secure enclave.

---

## 3. Security and Admin Safeguards

### 3.1 Immutable Audit Logging
- To prevent tampering and fraud, Louagi operates an append-only, tamper-resistant audit log.
- Every major action—such as logins, ride publishing, booking confirmations, cancellations, account suspensions, and administrative impersonations—is permanently recorded.
- Audit records cannot be modified, overwritten, or deleted by any user or administrator.

### 3.2 IP Allowlisting
- Access to administrative dashboards and sensitive backend commands is restricted via IP allowlisting, limiting access strictly to the internal operations (ops) network.

---

## 4. Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection Compliance)

### 4.1 Data Portability (Request Data Export)
- You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- Under settings, you can trigger a **Data Export**. This will generate a structured, machine-readable JSON file containing your profile info, bookings, delivery logs, and support tickets.

### 4.2 Account Deletion (Right to Be Forgotten)
- You may permanently delete your account at any time through the Profile settings.
- Deletion will deactivate your account and scrub your active profile from passenger searches.
- **Notice on Audit Logs**: Consistent with legal compliance, fraud prevention, and accounting requirements in Tunisia, records of past transactions and bookings will be retained within our immutable audit log for the required statutory period.
